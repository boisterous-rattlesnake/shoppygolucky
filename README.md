# ShoppyGoLucky 🍀

**ShoppyGoLucky** is a simple, lightweight, and private grocery list application designed for quick use on the go. It allows you to manage lists for up to four different stores simultaneously with color-coded tabs and smart features for the modern shopper.

## ✨ Key Features

* **Multi-Store Support:** Up to 4 customizable tabs. Rename them (e.g., "Trader Joe's", "Target", "Costco") and pick a unique color for each.
* **Smart Item Management:** * Tap an item to check it off.
    * **Move (➡):** Realized that milk is actually cheaper at the next store? Tap the arrow to transfer the item to the next tab.
    * **Delete (×):** Easily remove items you no longer need.
* **💡 Screen On Mode:** Toggle this to prevent your phone screen from dimming or locking while you are walking through the aisles.
* **💬 Text List:** (Mobile Only) Quickly share your remaining shopping list via SMS to a contact.
* **Privacy First:** All data is stored locally on your device's browser. No accounts, no tracking, and no cloud syncing required.

## 📱 How to "Install" on Your Phone

Since this is a single-file web app, you can make it behave like a real app on your phone:

### For iPhone (Safari)
1. Open the `ShoppyGoLucky.html` file in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an upward arrow) at the bottom of the screen.
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen."**
4. Tap **Add** in the top right corner. The 🍀 icon will now appear on your home screen!

### For Android (Chrome)
1. Open the `ShoppyGoLucky.html` file in Chrome.
2. Tap the **three dots (⋮)** in the top right corner.
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen."**
4. Follow the prompts to install it.

## 🛠️ Technical Details

* **Language:** HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript.
* **Storage:** `window.localStorage` (Persistent until browser cache is cleared).
* **API:** Uses the **Web Wake Lock API** to keep the screen active.
